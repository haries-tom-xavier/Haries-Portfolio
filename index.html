About Me

I'm Haries Tom Xavier, a passionate Textile Designer and emerging UI/UX enthusiast. My journey began with a drive to master the nuances of textile design—from the art of creating intricate sample designs to understanding the fine details of fabric sourcing, printing, dyeing, and weaving. With a Bachelor of Design from the National Institute of Fashion Technology and hands-on experience as an Assistant Fashion Designer, I’ve developed collections that blend traditional artistry with modern techniques, like block printing and tie-dye. With a foundation in creativity and a commitment to continuous learning, I am currently expanding my expertise into the digital world, focusing on UX design to craft experiences that resonate with users. In every project, I bring together curiosity, artistry, and a deep respect for quality. Whether it’s in textiles or user experiences, my goal is to design with purpose and a touch of innovation.
